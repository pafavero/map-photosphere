Map and Panorama/photosphere Viewer

Nowadays there is a boom in 360° panoramic images due to the ever-reducing cost of the technology, availability of software (stitching programs or applications for smart phones) and the possibility to embed them onto a web page. Even with my basic mobile phone (cost 200 €) I was able to generate a simple panoramic image!

This example shows how to connect a map to 360° panorama. Map and panorama are reciprocally related: the click on a icon on the map opens the correspondent panorama. And the navigation in the panorama update the rotation of the icon in the map.

I use the lightweight but powerfull Javascript library Viewer Pannellum to show the panorama
